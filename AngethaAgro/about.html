About Us

Angetha Agro Private Limited Company was established in 2020. We work with products of value derived from the Cannabis (Hemp) plant. Our aim is to develop products that allow individuals and businesses reduce their carbon footprint and lead a lifestyle which isn’t a burden on the planet.
We as humans need to acknowledge the fact that our consumption patterns and habits have a very profound effect on the planet and its well-being. Keeping in mind the damage already caused to the environment by us, we need to change our habits and consciously make choices that allow our planet to breathe.
Cannabis is a farmer-friendly crop that takes lesser time, space & water to grow and requires minimal to no use of pesticides. In the year 1938 Industrial Cannabis (Hemp) was called “The Billion-dollar crop” by the ‘Popular Mechanics’ magazine.
Different parts of the plant can be used to make over 50,000 products including, but not limited to, clothing, textiles, building material (wood, bricks, etc.), paper, bioplastics, biofuel, healthcare & nutrition. As a crop, it absorbs more Carbon dioxide per acre than any other plant, effectively making finished products made from Industrial Cannabis carbon neutral in nature.
Almost everything that we use every day to make our lives more convenient can be made using Industrial Cannabis, We at Angetha Agro are working towards making that a reality.
Our goal is to provide people with a wide range of Cannabis-derived products that serve as a better alternative to the existing ones and allow people to consciously choose nature-made remedies over man-made industries.
