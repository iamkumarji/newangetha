Benifits Of Hemp fabrics


Hemp isn’t a plant, it’s a multi-billion-dollar industry. Our vision is to create India’s largest Hemp farming community and use this plant as a tool to uplift Indian farmers & boost rural economy.
  • Pest Resistant Naturally pest-resistant so can be grown pesticide-free
  • long lasting Durable and long lasting hence saves money
  • Incredible Durability 4x the durability of other natural fibers
  • Environment friendly Sustainable and renewable, high-yield crop
  • Nature Extracted All-natural fiber and no synthetics involved
  • Absorbent The more you use hemp fabric and wash it the more malleable and softer it will become.
